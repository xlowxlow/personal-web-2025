About My AI Pathfinding

From Medical Devices to AI Innovation

During my ten-year career in medical devices, I promoted high-end imaging equipment at GE Healthcare and Neusoft, working closely with frontline medical institutions like Tianjin Medical University Cancer Institute. I witnessed firsthand how radiologists struggle in data floods and how resource inequality affects patient outcomes.

Technical Breakthroughs & Academic Achievements

Collaborating with Dr. Xiaonan Cui (Attending Physician, Radiology Dept., Tianjin Medical University Cancer Institute) and Dr. Sunyi Zheng (Assistant Researcher, Westlake University), our specialized LLM published in Radiology journal achieved F1 score of 0.58 and factual consistency of 88.9%, significantly outperforming general GPT-4o.
